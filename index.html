How compounds bind.

Each module operates at the prompt-transformation layer. No weight modification. No retraining. Reversible by default.
01 — Bind
Receptor Targeting
Modules identify specific inference pathways — attention heads, decay functions, sampling distributions — and bind without modifying base weights.
02 — Modulate
Parameter Adjustment
Bound compounds modulate behavior via prompt-layer transformations: temperature curves, chain-of-thought routing, attention compression.
03 — Express
Behavioral Shift
Effects manifest as measurable changes — reduced verbosity, tighter reasoning, improved factual grounding, novel association patterns.
04 — Clear
Reversible Offset
All compounds are session-scoped by default. No persistent modification. Effects clear on termination unless configured for persistence.
